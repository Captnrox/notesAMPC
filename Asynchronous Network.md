Fully meshed authenticated and private p2p channels. In the network the message delivery over the channels can should be assumed to be fully determined by the adversary. The adversary has the power to delay and change the arrival order of the messages. These messages always arrive eventually, that is, they can't be dropped, modified or eavesdropped when sent among honest nodes.

*Guo, B. et al. "From Speeding Dumbo: Pushing Asynchronous BFT Closer to Practice"*
